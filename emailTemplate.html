WHY GIVE THIS YEAR?

This year more than ever, the Wellington teachers are relying on the PTO funds to help them make this year special for your kids. But our kids are missing out because we can’t give what has been requested due to the severe drop in fundraising.

This year, we are asking families to consider donating $135 per child to the PTO Annual Fund so we can continue supporting our in-person and remote classrooms, teachers and students to the best of our ability during these trying times.

PROGRAMS WE CAN FUND WITH YOUR DONATION

VIRTUAL FIELD TRIPS & AUTHOR VISITS

Our teachers would love to supplement their curriculum with remote enrichment opportunities that allow our kids to meet well-known authors and enjoy high-quality virtual experiences to enhance their learning.

TEACHER SUPPORT & COMMUNITY EVENTS

Donations will provide ongoing support and appreciation for our childrens’ teachers (gifts, cards, treats, supplies) as well as creative events for our kids and families to build connections (magic show, storytime, bingo night).

ON-LINE SUBSCRIPTIONS

Every kindergarten student will receive a magazine and online subscription of Scholastic News this year to round out their science and social studies curricula.

LIBRARY PURCHASES

will allow them to take advantage of flash book sales and other opportunities to purchase inexpensive, high-quality books for our Wellington library.

WHERE WE ARE TODAY

We realize there are many challenges facing Wellington families, including economic hardship due to COVID-19, and humbly ask you to consider your own economic situation when making your donation.

Thank you for your consideration and support of our fantastic Wellington community!
